The Lasagna Recipe

Home

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish that consists of stacked layers of flat pasta sheets, typically filled with a savory mixture of ingredients such as meat, cheese, and sauce. The dish is traditionally baked in an oven to create a rich and hearty meal.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Ricotta Mixture:

  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Assembly:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

The Steps:

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Prepare the Ricotta Mixture
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
  5. Bake
  6. Serve
